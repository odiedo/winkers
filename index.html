ABOUT WINKERS CLUB

Experience the Ultimate Nightlife and Dining Destination

Winkers Club is the heartbeat of nightlife in Kenya and Uganda, offering an electrifying atmosphere where music, dance, and flavors collide. Our venues are designed to deliver unforgettable experiences with vibrant parties and exceptional dining.

From sizzling choma to gourmet dishes, our restaurant serves up culinary delights that keep you coming back. Join us for a night of fun, food, and celebration!
